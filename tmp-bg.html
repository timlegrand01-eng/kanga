The quick reference for keeping Kanga looking like Kanga across cards, stickers, signage, socials and print. When in doubt: dark green leaf, white roo, gold swoosh, Archivo, plenty of space.

Clear space & minimum size

Keep clear space around the logo equal to the height of the leaf glyph. Don't crowd it with other text or graphics. Minimum width for the full lockup: 26 mm in print / 150 px on screen. Below that, use the leaf glyph on its own.

The glyph on its own

Use as an app icon, favicon, social avatar, sticker or watermark. Always keep the gold swoosh — it's part of the mark. On Deep Green surfaces use the reverse glyph (white leaf, gold swoosh); on light surfaces the standard dark-green glyph. Superseded: the old cream-leaf/sage-vein treatment printed on the current card set retires when the cards are rebuilt on this mark.

Leaf Green is the primary. Gold is an accent only — stars, stems, small highlights and decorative leaf motifs; never a background or panel colour. Deep Green is for dark backgrounds and body headings. Cream is the warm alternative to plain white. Sage is the secondary for muted captions and small print only — never headlines, never on Deep Green (use Cream there). CMYK values are a SWOP coated conversion — for offset work, have the printer convert from RGB with their own profile. The logo leaf runs its own darker green, #155724 (approx C90 M40 Y95 K45), with the shared Gold swoosh — logo art only, never a UI or background colour. Typography

Archivo

Display & headings · weights 400–900

Used for the wordmark, headlines and buttons. SemiBold to ExtraBold (600–800) for headings, Regular for the "Gardening" half of the wordmark.

Inter

Body text · weights 400–600

Used for paragraphs, captions and everything you actually read. Keep body text at a comfortable size with generous line spacing.

Do & don't

  • Keep the leaf, the roo and the gold swoosh together
  • Give the logo room to breathe
  • Use Cream or White behind the logo
  • Use the reverse glyph (white leaf) on Deep Green
  • Don't recolour the leaf or drop the gold swoosh
  • Don't stretch, squash or rotate the mark
  • Don't add shadows, gradients or outlines
  • Don't place it on busy, low-contrast photos

QR & print production

  • QR codes: minimum 18 mm printed, 4-module quiet zone on a White or Cream panel. Deep Green modules are fine. Decode-verify the final composed artwork at 100% and again at 50% scale before every print order.
  • Artwork: 3 mm bleed, keep content 6 mm inside the bleed edge. 300 dpi minimum; keep text vector (or flatten lossless) wherever possible.
  • Type floor for print: 6 pt (5.5 pt only in vector artwork).
