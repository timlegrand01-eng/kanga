Common questions

Office plant maintenance - common questions

What does office plant maintenance include?
Watering and feeding, cleaning and grooming, pest management, and replacing any plant that isn't thriving, on a regular schedule with the same person each visit.
Do you service offices in the CBD?
Kanga focuses on the south-east Melbourne business parks (Dandenong, Moorabbin, Clayton, Mulgrave, Cranbourne). CBD sites can be discussed, but the south-east is where we're based and most efficient.
Can you take over an existing plant display?
Yes. Taking over tired or declining displays is common. We assess what's there, treat and revive what we can, and replace what can't be saved.
How often do office plants need servicing?
Most offices are serviced fortnightly or monthly depending on the plants, light and positions. The schedule is set to keep the display healthy.
Are replacements included?
Replacement of plants that aren't thriving is part of keeping the display presentable. We'll confirm how that works for your site in the quote.
