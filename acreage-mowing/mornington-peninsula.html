Acreage · Mornington Peninsula

The right machine for peninsula blocks

The Mornington Peninsula is lifestyle-block country: large coastal gardens through Mornington, Mt Martha, Safety Beach and Dromana, and bigger rural-residential and hobby-farm properties up in the Red Hill, Main Ridge and Merricks hinterland. These blocks take a proper machine and a reliable routine, not a push mower and a free weekend.

Kanga runs a commercial ride-on to keep peninsula acreage lawns, house surrounds and paddock edges even and tidy through the growing season. The terrain undulates and the coastal exposure drives strong spring growth, so the schedule is matched to how fast the grass is actually moving, with a fixed quote and the same operator every visit.

Local questions

Mornington Peninsula - common questions

Which peninsula areas do you cover?
Mornington, Mt Martha, Safety Beach and Dromana on the coast, plus the Red Hill, Main Ridge, Merricks and Balnarring hinterland. Ask about your property.
Can you handle large rural blocks and paddocks?
Yes. A commercial ride-on plus slashing handles large lawns, paddock edges and rougher areas across peninsula blocks.
How often should a peninsula block be mown?
It depends on the season. Coastal growth is strong in spring and slower in the cooler months. The schedule is set to suit the property.
