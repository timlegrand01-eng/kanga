First impressions count

Your grounds are the first thing clients and tenants see

A commercial property's outdoor areas set the tone before anyone walks through the door. Overgrown frontages, tired beds and uneven lawns read as neglect; sharp, consistent presentation reads as a business that has its act together. Kanga keeps commercial sites looking the part, on a schedule you can rely on.

You get a fixed monthly fee, a schedule that fits around your operating hours, green-waste removal included, and the same operator every visit. No rotating crews, no surprise invoices — just consistent, professional presentation and a direct line to the person doing the work.

Common questions

Commercial grounds maintenance — common questions

What does commercial grounds maintenance include?
Mowing and edging, hedging and pruning, garden-bed maintenance and weed control, and green-waste removal — all under one fixed monthly fee, scheduled to suit your site.
Can you work outside business hours to avoid disruption?
Scheduling is set to suit the property — including quieter times where access and noise matter. Let us know your constraints at the site walkthrough.
How is commercial grounds maintenance priced?
After a free site walkthrough you receive a fixed monthly quote based on the site's size, access and frequency, with no call-out charges and green-waste included.
Are you insured to work on commercial sites?
Yes — $20M public and products liability, fully insured and police-checked, with a Certificate of Currency available on request.
Do you take on one-off commercial clean-ups?
Kanga focuses on recurring maintenance rather than one-off jobs, which is what keeps a site consistently presentable. Get in touch and we'll talk through what your site needs.
