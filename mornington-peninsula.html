Acreage · Casey & Cardinia

Big outer-south-east blocks, done properly

Out past the housing growth, the Casey and Cardinia fringe is full of rural-residential and lifestyle blocks — established acreage around Clyde, Officer and Pakenham, and bigger hobby-farm and hills properties through Beaconsfield Upper, Nar Nar Goon and the Cardinia ranges. These are the blocks where a domestic mower simply can't keep up over a growing season.

Kanga runs a commercial ride-on to keep outer-south-east acreage lawns, house surrounds and paddock edges even and under control, with slashing for the longer and rougher areas. You get a fixed quote, scheduling matched to how fast your grass grows, and the same operator every visit.
