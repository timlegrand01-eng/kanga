About Kanga Gardening

A local, owner-operated grounds-maintenance business

Kanga Gardening is run from Edithvale by its owner — who is on site for the work, not a call-centre dispatching a different crew each week. The business focuses on recurring maintenance for the properties that need reliability, presentation and the right equipment: body corporate and strata complexes, commercial and industrial sites, acreage and lifestyle properties, and office plant displays.

The idea behind Kanga is simple. Most managers and property owners don't want to think about the grounds at all — they want one reliable contractor who turns up, keeps everything presentable, and doesn't create complaints, surprise invoices or admin. So that's how the business runs: a fixed monthly fee, a schedule that suits the site, green-waste removal included, and a short service report after every visit.

How Kanga runs

Recurring maintenance, held to one standard

Kanga works on recurring programs rather than one-off jobs — that's what keeps a site consistently presentable and lets the same operator get to know it. Every property is on a set schedule that suits it, with green waste removed each visit and disposed of responsibly, and a short service report so managers and committees always have a record.

Because one person quotes the work, does the work and stands behind it, accountability doesn't get diluted across a rotating crew. If something's not right, you deal directly with the owner — and it gets fixed.
