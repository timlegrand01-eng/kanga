Body Corporate · Bayside & Kingston

Local to the bay, and it shows

Kanga is based in Edithvale, right in the middle of this strip, so Bayside and Kingston complexes get the fastest response and the most regular presence. The bayside suburbs are full of villa-unit blocks, walk-up apartments and small owners corporations near the foreshore. Exactly the common-area work Kanga is built around.

The coastal position matters for the gardens. Sandy soils through the Edithvale-Mordialloc belt drain fast and need the right feeding, and salt-laden wind off the bay is hard on exposed beds and hedges. Lawns here are typically couch or buffalo that thrive in summer and slow right down over winter, so the maintenance schedule is matched to the season rather than run on autopilot.
