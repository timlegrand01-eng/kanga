Why Kanga

The accountability of one operator, not a national call-centre

What managers want from a grounds contractor isn't the cheapest quote. It's one less job on their plate.

Ride-on capability

A commercial ride-on means large blocks, common areas and acreage get mowed quickly and tidily, a turnaround smaller mow-and-go operators can't match.

The same operator, every visit

You deal with the same person every visit, with a direct line to the owner. Not a rotating crew, not a call-centre, not a different face each week.

Fixed fee, seasonal reporting

A set monthly fee with green waste included and no call-out fees, plus seasonal condition reports so you're never chasing confirmation.
