Low-fuss, well-presented sites

Tidy industrial grounds, without the admin

Industrial and warehouse sites still need to present well (for tenants, buyers, councils and insurers) but they're often the last thing a facility manager has time for. Kanga keeps frontages, fencelines, car parks and yard surrounds under control on a set schedule, so the site stays tidy without you having to think about it.

You get a fixed monthly fee, green-waste removal included, ride-on capability for larger open areas, and the same operator every visit. One point of contact, one predictable cost, and a tidy site, across the Dandenong South, Pakenham and Cranbourne industrial precincts.

Common questions

Industrial grounds maintenance - common questions

What does industrial grounds maintenance include?
Ride-on mowing and slashing, fenceline and hard-edge weed control, frontage and bed tidy-ups, and green-waste removal, under one fixed monthly fee.
Which areas do you cover for industrial sites?
The south-east industrial precincts (primarily Dandenong South, Pakenham and Cranbourne) and surrounding areas. Check the service map or get in touch.
Can you handle large frontages and yards?
Yes. A commercial ride-on means larger open areas and verges are mown and slashed quickly and tidily.
Are you insured for work on industrial sites?
Yes. $20M public and products liability, fully insured and police-checked, with a Certificate of Currency on request.
Do you offer weed control along fencelines and hardstand?
Yes. Fencelines, kerbs, car parks and hard edges are kept clear and tidy as part of the regular schedule.
