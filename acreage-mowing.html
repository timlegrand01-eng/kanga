Common questions

Acreage mowing - common questions

Do you mow acreage and large rural blocks?
Yes. A commercial ride-on handles large lawns, lifestyle blocks and paddock-edge areas across the Mornington Peninsula and outer south-east Melbourne.
Which areas do you cover for acreage mowing?
Primarily the Mornington Peninsula (Mornington, Mt Martha, Red Hill, Main Ridge) and the Casey and Cardinia outer south-east (Officer, Pakenham, Clyde). Check the service map or ask.
How often should a large block be mown?
It depends on the season and how fast the grass grows. Typically more often in spring and less in the cooler months. The schedule is set to suit your property.
How is acreage mowing priced?
After looking at the property you receive a fixed quote based on the area, terrain and frequency. No call-out charges.
Can you handle longer grass and rougher areas?
Yes. Slashing keeps longer grass, paddock edges and rougher areas under control alongside the lawn mowing.
