Indoor Plants · South-East Business Parks

Indoor plant care, where the offices actually are

Most indoor-plant companies chase the CBD towers and leave the south-east business parks as an afterthought — yet Dandenong, Moorabbin, Clayton, Mulgrave, Notting Hill and Cranbourne are full of offices, showrooms and reception areas with plant displays that need looking after. That's exactly where Kanga is based and most efficient.

Office and reception plants only look good when someone reliable is genuinely maintaining them — watering to the right level, feeding, cleaning, managing pests early, and swapping out anything that isn't thriving. Kanga services SE business-park offices on a regular schedule with the same person each visit, so displays stay healthy rather than slowly declining after the initial install.
