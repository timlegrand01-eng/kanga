Common questions

Body corporate gardening - common questions

What does body corporate gardening include?
Common-area mowing (ride-on or push), hedging and pruning, garden-bed maintenance and weed control, and green-waste removal, all under one fixed monthly fee, with a service report after each visit.
How much does body corporate gardening cost in Melbourne?
Every complex is different (block size, frequency, access and scope all vary), so pricing is quoted after a free site walkthrough. You receive a fixed monthly fee with no call-out charges and green-waste removal included, so the cost is predictable for budgeting.
How often should body corporate gardens be maintained?
Most owners corporations are maintained weekly, fortnightly or monthly depending on the site and the season. Lawns and common areas usually need more attention in the warmer months and less through winter; the schedule is set to suit the property.
Are you insured for body corporate and strata work?
Yes. Kanga Gardening carries $20M public and products liability and is fully insured, police-checked and a White Card holder. A Certificate of Currency is available on request for the committee's records.
Do you provide reports to the owners corporation manager?
Yes. A short service report after each visit records what was done and flags anything that needs attention, so the manager and committee never have to chase confirmation that the work happened.
