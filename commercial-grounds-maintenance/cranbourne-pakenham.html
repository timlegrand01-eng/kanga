Commercial · Cranbourne–Pakenham

Keeping the corridor's new commercial sites sharp

The Princes Highway and M1 corridor from Cranbourne out to Pakenham is one of Melbourne's busiest growth areas — new retail centres, bulky-goods showrooms, medical and business estates opening alongside the housing. These are exposed, high-visibility sites where presentation directly affects how the business is judged by a steady stream of new customers.

Kanga maintains commercial frontages and surrounds across the corridor on a fixed-fee schedule, with ride-on capability for larger open areas, green-waste removal included, and the same operator every visit. Predictable cost, consistent presentation, and one point of contact for the whole site.
